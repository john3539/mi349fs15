Space Crusader

Role Description

Space Crusader is a Turn-Based strategy game that allows the player to pick a squad of soldiers and head-to-head against their friends in a battle of wits. The Player will have to learn how to keep a balanced team in each level so, that your friend doesn't find a way to bomb your base.

Project Length: 5 weeks

Role: Gameplay Programmer
